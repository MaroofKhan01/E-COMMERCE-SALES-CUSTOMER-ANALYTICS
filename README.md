
# Olist E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to understand **sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends**. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), customer segmentation, and interactive visualizations to generate actionable insights.

## Key Highlights

* Analyzed sales trends and revenue growth over time.
* Identified top-performing product categories.
* Performed customer segmentation using RFM analysis.
* Studied the impact of delivery performance on customer satisfaction.
* Explored regional sales distribution and customer locations.

## Tools & Technologies

* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* **SQL**
* **Jupyter Notebook**

## Key Insights

* Revenue showed strong growth over the analysis period.
* Health & Beauty, Sports & Leisure, and Bed/Bath/Table were the top-selling categories.
* Loyal customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
* Late deliveries resulted in lower customer review scores.
* São Paulo generated the highest sales among all regions.
